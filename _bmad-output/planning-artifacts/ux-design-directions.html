lingosips — Design Directions
6 visual and interaction approaches for the lingosips interface. Each direction is a complete vision — different layout, information hierarchy, and interaction pattern. Click any direction to explore the full mockup.
Direction 1
Linear Tool
Classic two-panel tool layout. Left sidebar with navigation and deck list. Main area shows card creation + preview + stats. Closest to Linear/VSCode information density.
Sidebar navDenseTool-like
Direction 2
Dashboard Hero
Icon-only slim sidebar. Main area split: large card creation zone on the left, review queue + recent cards on the right. Creation is the hero element.
Slim sidebarCreation-firstDual panel
Direction 3
Split Panel
Top navigation bar. Body splits equally: creation on the left, practice session on the right. Both modes always visible. Most literal interpretation of "continuous learning."
Top nav50/50 splitAlways-on
Direction 4
Focus Flow
Top navigation, centered content column (max 680px). Large prominent creation input. Card result expands below. Queue shown as a subtle banner. Maximum focus, minimum chrome.
CenteredMinimal chromeProgressive
Direction 5
Speak Mode
Full-viewport practice session mockup — specifically the speak mode with syllable-level pronunciation feedback. Shows the novel UX pattern in detail.
Full viewportSyllable feedbackPractice
Direction 6
Card Browser
Wider sidebar with deck list + search. Main area is a card grid browser with a detail panel. Emphasizes the deck/collection management experience. Good for power users with large decks.
Wide sidebarCard gridCollection-first
